### 👨‍💻 About Me

I’m a **Senior Software Engineer** based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with over **5 years of professional experience in Laravel** and **3+ years in Vue.js**. Being a full-stack developer allows me to architect robust, client-facing applications, but my true passion lies in **backend engineering**. 

I specialize in building secure, scalable backends, designing RESTful APIs, optimizing complex databases, and maintaining production-ready systems that do all the heavy lifting. I love designing systems that are light yet powerful, distributed yet synchronized, and beautiful yet highly effective.

---

### 🚀 What I'm Up To

- 🔭 **Currently Building:** High-performance REST APIs, SaaS wrappers, and exploring WhatsApp API automation.
- 💼 **Professional Focus:** Advanced authentication (Sanctum/JWT), asynchronous processing (WebSockets/Queues), and system optimization. 
- 🌱 **Currently Learning:** Deepening my knowledge in VueX, Pinia, and exploring React Native.
